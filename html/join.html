Join Runnin’ Wreck

All paces welcome. Show up, say hi, and run with us.

Three Easy Steps

  1. Sign Up!
    You should be a current Georgia Tech student with at least 6 credit hours. Grad and undergrad are welcome.
    Register with CRC Club Sports here
  2. Become Eligible
    Submit a completed physical form and have it approved. If you need help obtaining or submitting the form, DM an Officer and we’ll assist you.
    Submit your Physical Here!
  3. Come to a practice
    Pick any session on our practice schedule and introduce yourself when you arrive. We’ll help you find a pace group.
  4. Join the GroupMe
    Password: Fall2025

Your First Practice

  • Bring: running shoes, water bottle.
  • Meet spot: Meet us outside the CRC M/W/F and at McCamish Pavilion T/Th!
  • Pace groups: We split into groups so everyone has someone to run with.
  • Weather: We run in light rain; lightning/heat may shift plans—watch announcements.

Dues & Member Perks

Semester dues help fund club operations. Membership typically includes:

  • Subsidized race entries & shared travel
  • Access to the competitive NIRCA team
  • Team Singlet
  • Access to Club socials and events

Exact amount and payment links are shared at the start of each semester.
